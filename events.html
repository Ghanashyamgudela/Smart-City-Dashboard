City Marathon
Sports Expected Attendance: 10,000+
Date & Time

June 13, 2023 at 8:00 AM

Location

Central Park, New York

Organizer

Description

The annual City Marathon is back for its 25th year! Join thousands of runners from around the world for this iconic race through the heart of the city.

The marathon course takes participants through scenic routes, historic landmarks, and vibrant neighborhoods.

Event Details
  • Full Marathon (26.2 miles)
  • Half Marathon (13.1 miles)
  • 10K Run
  • 5K Fun Run/Walk

Registration is required. Visit the official website for more details.
